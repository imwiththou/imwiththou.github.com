    SIMPLICITY IS THE ULTIMATE SOPHISTICATION

Hi I’m Stephen and I am living at Singapore currently, a tiny island lying in the corner of southeastern Asia. I am still achieving my degree in university and sooner or later, I might be getting myself as a bio-medical engineer in relevant industries. Despite of that, I am also passionate and crazy about graphic designing and photography, which was an leisure hobby since high school time. I think design is the channel that changes our way of thinking on the questions like how we can make life better, or how we can make life easier. Half a year earlier I started to put my interest onto front-end designing, I was keen in pixel and visual perfection. Well perhaps I am not good at hand-drawing, though — or it is just terrible, I tend to strongly believe in imagination, a tool that everyone have. This tool, is the power of changing life.
